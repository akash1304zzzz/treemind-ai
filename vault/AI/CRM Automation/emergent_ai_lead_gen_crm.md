# Emergent AI Lead Gen CRM

This note summarizes a custom, code-free lead generation and CRM tool built on **Emergent** that helps find and manage leads without relying on paid services like Apollo or Clay.

### Key Takeaways:

* **No-Code Construction**: Built entirely by prompting the Emergent AI agent with a description of the desired database schema and workflow.
* **Lead Ingestion Source**: Connects to Gmail to read alerts from **Google Alerts** (built on public data, requiring no external APIs).
* **Automated Data Scraping**: AI agents scrape Google Alerts data to pull:
  * Company Name
  * Source Link
  * Date of mention
  * Relevant alert snippet
* **Intelligent Lead Scoring**: Automatically scores each lead on a scale of 1 to 10 based on ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) match, keywords, and location.
* **Matched Context**: Generates a one-line explanation for each lead's match reason.
* **CRM & Dashboard**: Sends daily digests of the top matched leads and provides a searchable CRM dashboard.
