## 🔍 Platform Feature Breakdown

TradeFinder.in acts as a real-time scanner. The video displays the following primary modules:

### 1. Option Apex & Option Clock
- **Purpose**: Tracks live options flows, volume anomalies, and momentum spikes.
- **Utility**: Helps options buyers identify where institutional block trades or sudden retail surges are happening.

### 2. Swing Spectrum
- **Purpose**: Scans historical price ranges to identify breakout candidates for swing trading.
- **Utility**: Focuses on patterns that indicate mid-to-long term direction shifts (e.g. cup-and-handle breakout or consolidation ranges).

### 3. Insider Strategy & Sector Scope
- **Purpose**: Isolates sector-specific momentum.
- **Utility**: Trade setups are selected only when the underlying sector (e.g. Nifty IT, Nifty Bank) displays strong capital inflows.

### 4. Market Pulse
- **Purpose**: Real-time sentiment gauge.
- **Utility**: Prevents entering long positions when the overall market index (Nifty/Sensex) displays bearish price action.
