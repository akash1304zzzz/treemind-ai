- **Key Features**: YouTube allows users to enjoy videos and music, upload original content, and share it with others globally. 
- **Content Sharing**: Users can share content with friends, family, and the world. 
- **Original Content Upload**: Users can upload their own original content to the platform. 
- **Global Reach**: Content can be shared and accessed by people all over the world.
