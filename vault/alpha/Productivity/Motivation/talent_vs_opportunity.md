- Talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. This means that many people with great potential are not given the chance to succeed. 
- Success is often about getting the right chance at the right time, rather than just relying on talent. 
- We should create more opportunities to support hidden talent and believe in people before the world does. 
- Every dream deserves a chance to shine, and we should strive to make opportunity universal.
